One-Click Capture
from Any Website
Amazon, Pinterest, competitor stores, brand sites — click once and the URL is captured automatically. Setup takes 60 seconds.
1 Update the URL in this file
Open pavoi-bookmarklet.html in a text editor and find:

const CAPTURE_URL = 'https://YOUR_USERNAME.github.io/…';

Replace YOUR_USERNAME with your GitHub username and pavoi-inspire with your repo name. Save and reload this page in your browser.
2 Drag to your bookmarks bar
Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible (Chrome: ⌘⇧B / Ctrl+Shift+B  ·  Safari: View → Show Favorites Bar), then drag the button below into it:

3 Using it
The flow
01Browse to any product page, Amazon listing, Pinterest pin, or competitor site
02Click PAVOI Capture in your bookmarks bar
03PAVOI Inspire opens in a new tab — the page URL is pre-filled
04The bookmarklet also tries to grab the og:image (main product photo) automatically
05Add your note and submit — done
Pro tip — Right-click for a cleaner image On most sites, right-click the main product image → "Copy Image Address" → paste into the Image URL field for a sharper, unpadded capture.
4 Instagram & TikTok (mobile)
In-app browsers on iOS don't pass URLs to the Share Sheet. Use the paste workaround:
Mobile workflow
01Tap the share icon in Instagram or TikTok → Copy Link
02Open PAVOI Capture from your Home Screen
03Tap Add URL → tap the 📋 Paste button next to Source URL
04Add your note and submit
